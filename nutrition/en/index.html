Why this site?

Eating well is needlessly hard today.

Anyone trying to eat a balanced diet faces supermarket shelves full of ultra-processed products – sugar, additives, clever marketing – and an internet locked in ideological trench warfare. Neither helps you cook dinner on a Wednesday night.

The big levers sit with policy and retail: honest products, fair prices, real alternatives. Until those move, this site lowers the second barrier – knowledge.

In plain language, we explain how to put together meals that actually nourish you – with few ingredients, little time, and a normal budget. Step by step, across five stages, and you decide how far to go.

With meat or without, quick or elaborate, athletically ambitious or simply fed and healthy: the plan adapts to you, not the other way around.

Our tone

You don't need to become an expert. We help you improve from where you are today.
